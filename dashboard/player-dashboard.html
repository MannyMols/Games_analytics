Five prioritised actions across game design, UA, and LiveOps — each tied to a specific finding from the analysis.
🎮
Fix the Day 0 Experience
37% of players who attempt the first boss fail to defeat it and churn immediately. Reduce difficulty, improve mechanic telegraphing, or add a retry tip UI. This is the single highest-impact lever — it improves D7, D30, and revenue simultaneously.
Game Design
⚔️
Surface Side Content Earlier
Arena, Gauntlet, and Raids adopters retain at 2–3× the rate of non-adopters. Only 6–18% of players discover these modes organically. In-game quests after Chapter 2–3, or a LiveOps event tied to Arena unlock, could significantly lift D30.
Game Design · LiveOps
📊
Optimise UA Channel Mix
Apple Search Ads leads on LTV proxy (£6.20 avg IAP). Organic and Influencer lead on D7/D30 retention. TikTok Ads is weakest on both. Scale high-quality sources and audit TikTok Ads creatives and audience targeting before further spend increases.
User Acquisition
🧠
Use ML Clusters for LiveOps
KMeans behavioural clustering rediscovers Whale, Spender, Grinder, Casual, and Churned segments from raw data. Deploy these as LiveOps targeting audiences — personalised bundles for high-value players, difficulty tuning for Casuals, win-back campaigns for Churned-early.
LiveOps · Data Science
🎁
Roll Out the Legendary Chest Variant
The higher drop-rate chest variant improves D7 retention by +10.4% relative and payer conversion by +8.2%. Make it the new default, monitor D60 LTV and game-balance closely, and follow up with a second test exploring the optimal drop-rate level.
LiveOps · Experimentation
💰
Prioritise Whale & Spender Retention
138 Whales average £428 IAP each. 867 Spenders average £49. These two segments account for the vast majority of total revenue despite being under 3.5% of the player base. Exclusive seasonal content, VIP perks, and targeted offers should protect and grow this cohort.
